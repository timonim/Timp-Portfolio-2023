I'm Tim Portnoy, a product designer from Tel Aviv, Israel. I create designs that are both visually stunning and incredibly functional, specializing in working with complex products and design systems. My skills include UI/UX, brand design, typography, AI image generation, marketing, graphic design, and 3D, and I use tools like Figma, Stable Diffusion, Blender, Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, Procreate, and more.

My goal is to create designs that solve real-world problems and exceed expectations. Whether it's designing an intuitive app interface or a comprehensive brand identity, I'm passionate about crafting beautiful, functional designs that are tailored to the unique needs of each project.

Asides design, I love languages, jazz and history podcasts.
