Branding, Logo & 3D

Locu

Grasshopper protein supplements

Locu was my bachelor's thesis project done in collaboration with Hargol, an Israeli food tech company. The thesis, "Branding Insects: Transitioning Pest to Food" involved research, market analysis, brief writing, branding, 3D modeling, simulation and rendering.

The gummy series depicts an idyllic nature scene that features complementary colors to the packaging. The product aims to appeal towards casual customers looking to enhance their nutrition with a tasty, daily snack.
